FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I find volunteer opportunities on your website?

A: To find volunteer opportunities on our website, simply click on the opportunities close to you, and sign up for them!

Q: Is there a minimum age requirement to volunteer through your website?

A: The minimum age requirement to volunteer through our website may vary depending on the specific opportunity. Some opportunities may have a minimum age of 16, while others may have a minimum age of 18 those involving governement organization volunteering. Make sure to check the age requirement for each opportunity before applying.

Q: Can I volunteer on a regular basis through your website?

A: Yes, there are many opportunities available on our website that are ongoing and allow for regular volunteer commitments. You can filter the opportunities by time commitment to find opportunities that match your availability.

Q: Is there a limit to the number of volunteer opportunities I can sign up for?

A: There is no limit to the number of volunteer opportunities you can sign up for through our website, however, it is important to make sure you can commit to the time and effort required for each opportunity before applying.

Q: How can I get a certificate of completion for my volunteer hours?

A: Once you complete a volunteer opportunity, you can contact the organization to request a certificate of completion for your volunteer hours. Some organizations may provide a certificate automatically or include it in the volunteer orientation.
